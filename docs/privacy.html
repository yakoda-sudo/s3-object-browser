Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

S3 Mac Browser is a local macOS application for managing object storage. We do not operate a backend service for the app. The app connects only to storage endpoints that you configure (for example, AWS S3, S3‑compatible services, or Azure Blob Storage).

Information We Collect

The app does not require sign‑up and does not send personal data to us. Any connection profiles, keys, or settings you enter are stored locally on your Mac for your own use.

How We Use Information

The app uses your locally stored settings only to connect to the endpoints you configure and to display results in the app. We do not use your data for advertising or tracking.

Network Requests

The app communicates directly with your configured storage endpoints to list buckets/containers, fetch object metadata, and perform actions you initiate. We do not proxy or inspect this traffic.

Third‑Party Services

The app does not include third‑party analytics SDKs. If you click external links (for example, to GitHub or the Mac App Store), those services have their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

You can remove saved profiles or uninstall the app at any time to delete locally stored data.
